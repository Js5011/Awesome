#632 Commanding your brother/sister to do something for you - 1000 Awesome Things

May 22, 2024November 19, 2009 Jujhar Seehra

Commanding your brother on what to do. Have you ever sat down after a long day, being all tired and stuff, and told your younger sibling to do something for you…AND HE DOES IT? That is just pure joy. Your younger sibling listening to you is a rare occurrence, let alone doing something for you willingly. At most times, I can’t even convince him to let me sleep for 10 more minutes or to get a book and start reading, so it is a big deal. Moments like these show that no matter how rude your sibling may seem, they always care for you and will help you in your moment of need. It truly brings a smile to my face realizing how much my brother really cares for me. Plus, the soothing feeling of laying in bed while your brother brings a lemonade freshly poured into the glass and getting your phone so you can watch YouTube Shorts; that’s just…

AWESOME!
